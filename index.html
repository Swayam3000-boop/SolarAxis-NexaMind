How It Works

From rooftop to royalty
in three simple steps

SolarAxis bridges the gap between solar prosumers and the grid — making clean energy profitable and transparent for everyone.

Install & Connect
Install Your Solar System
Register your rooftop PV installation on SolarAxis. Our certified installers connect your system to the platform and configure your net-metering smart meter for two-way energy tracking.
Generate & Export
Generate Solar, Export Surplus
Your solar panels generate power throughout the day. Self-consume what you need; the surplus automatically flows back to the grid and is tracked in real time on your SolarAxis dashboard.
Track & Earn
Earn Monthly Royalties
Every unit exported is automatically reconciled with your DISCOM. Monthly royalty statements are generated and payments credited directly — no paperwork, no delays, full transparency.
For Grid Operators
GridAxis — Complete DISCOM Command Centre
DISCOMs and grid operators get the full GridAxis suite: real-time prosumer inflow tracking, locality-wise energy mapping, frequency monitoring, automated royalty billing, and fault detection — all from a single dashboard.
Platform Features

Everything you need to
manage solar at scale

Built for India's grid — designed for the realities of net metering, DISCOM compliance, and distributed rooftop solar.

Live Energy Monitoring
Real-time generation, consumption, and export data updated every 2 seconds. Never miss a peak or fault.
Automated Royalty Billing
Monthly net-metering reconciliation and royalty payments fully automated — no manual ledger entries needed.
AI-Powered Forecasting
Machine learning models predict your next 7 days of generation using local weather, panel degradation, and historical output.
Locality Energy Heatmaps
Zone-level energy generation maps for grid operators — identify underperforming localities and overloaded feeders instantly.
Smart Fault Detection
Automatic alerts for panel faults, meter timeouts, feeder overloads, and frequency deviations — before they escalate.
Environmental Analytics
Track CO₂ avoided, trees equivalent, Renewable Energy Certificates earned, and your lifetime environmental footprint.
